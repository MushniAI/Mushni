# Safety and Compliance

Mushni encourages responsible AI-assisted content creation. This document provides practical guidance for creators using AI tools in public workflows.

## Human Review

Creators should review AI-generated content before publishing or sharing it. Review is especially important for:

- Factual claims
- Legal, medical, financial, or political content
- Depictions of real people
- Sensitive or age-restricted content
- Content intended for children
- Commercial publishing

## Intellectual Property

Avoid using prompts or workflows that ask an AI system to copy protected work, imitate a living artist in a misleading way, or reproduce recognizable characters without permission.

When building a commercial project, keep records of:

- Source materials
- Licensed assets
- AI-generated drafts
- Human edits
- Final publishing decisions

## Platform Rules

Creators are responsible for following the rules of the platforms where they publish content. Before publishing, check:

- Content policy
- Age restrictions
- Disclosure requirements
- Copyright and trademark rules
- Monetization rules

## Transparency

When appropriate, disclose that AI tools were used in the creative process. This is especially helpful for commercial work, collaborative projects, and platforms with AI labeling policies.

## Prohibited Uses

Do not use Mushni workflows to create, distribute, or promote content that is illegal, abusive, deceptive, exploitative, or intended to harm others.
