# AI Image-to-Video Workflow with Mushni

This workflow explains how to plan short AI videos from a source image, visual reference, or storyboard panel.

## Goal

Turn a stable visual idea into a short video clip with controlled motion, identity, and publishing format.

## Steps

1. Choose the source image or visual reference.
2. Write a short motion prompt.
3. Add negative prompt details for unwanted motion or visual artifacts.
4. Choose aspect ratio, duration, and frame rate.
5. Generate a short preview.
6. Review motion, identity consistency, camera movement, and visual quality.
7. Regenerate only when the prompt or source image needs improvement.
8. Save the final clip with clear project notes.

## Recommended Mushni Path

- Use ImgAI.one to prepare visual references and storyboard frames.
- Use HiAI.one for image-to-video and short video generation workflows.
- Keep prompts simple when testing motion, then add detail after the first preview.

## Quality Checklist

- The main subject remains recognizable.
- Motion supports the scene rather than distracting from it.
- Duration and frame rate fit the publishing channel.
- The result is reviewed before public use.
- Rights and platform rules are checked before publishing.
